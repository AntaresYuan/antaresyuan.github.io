Univision

Highlighting Minority Challenges through Art & Connection

Welcome to Univision, a student organization dedicated to fostering understanding and awareness by addressing challenges faced by minority communities through art and gallery exhibitions. We aim to create a community for students to bond while shedding light on important social issues.

Our Goal

We, Univision, are dedicated to highlighting the challenges faced by minority communities and offering fresh perspectives through art and gallery exhibitions. Our mission is to foster understanding and awareness by showcasing diverse viewpoints and experiences.

Every school year, all departments work towards creating a single project with a specific minority group as the focus. Around spring quarter, exhibitions will be opened to the public, raising awareness of the challenges faced by the focused group.

Not only do we focus on social issues, but we also create a community for students to connect and support one another.
