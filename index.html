About Us

Welcome to Univision, a student organization dedicated to fostering understanding and awareness by addressing challenges faced by minority communities through art and gallery exhibitions. Our goal is to showcase diverse perspectives, strengthen community bonds, and promote awareness of critical social issues.

Our Mission

We, Univision, are dedicated to highlighting the challenges faced by minority communities and offering fresh perspectives through art and gallery exhibitions. Our mission is to foster understanding and awareness by showcasing diverse viewpoints and experiences.

Each school year, we focus on a single minority group. In spring quarter, we organize a public exhibition to raise awareness of the group's challenges. Univision is not only a platform to explore social issues but also a supportive community for students to connect and grow together.
